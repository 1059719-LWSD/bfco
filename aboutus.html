home · about us

we design quietly.

bernoulli & friends co. is a small studio focused on minimal, well-made products. we believe in clarity, longevity and tools that invite curiosity.

est. 2025

mission

build simple instruments for everyday use — objects that feel inevitable, work reliably, and age gracefully.

• fewer features, better defaults
• considerate materials & repairability
• software that stays fast and kind

principles

01 — clarity

interfaces should explain themselves. the best manual is none at all.

02 — calm

color, motion and sound are used sparingly to reduce noise.

03 — continuity

our products are designed to last. we value stability over trends.
