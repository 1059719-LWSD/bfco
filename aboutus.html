we design quietly.

Bernoulli and Friends Co. is a small studio based in Sandwich, Massachusetts. We build instruments that keep quality, functionality, and minimal as a focus.

mission

Build simple instruments for everyday use — objects that feel inevitable, work reliably, and age gracefully.

  • Fewer features, better defaults
  • Considerate & high quality materials
  • Razor‑sharp precision with every build

principles

01 — clarity

Items should explain themselves. The best manual is none at all.

02 — calm

Color, feel, and sound are used sparingly for a tranquil experience.

03 — continuity

Our products are designed to last.
